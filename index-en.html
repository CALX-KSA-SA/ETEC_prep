School Accreditation: National Mandate 2026

School accreditation in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia is a mandatory regulatory requirement under the authority of the Education & Training Evaluation Commission (ETEC). Accreditation is a core mechanism for ensuring educational quality, institutional accountability, and alignment with national education priorities under Vision 2030.
